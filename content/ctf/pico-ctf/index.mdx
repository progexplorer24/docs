# picoCTF

**picoCTF** is a beginner-friendly cybersecurity competition designed to help participants learn and practice a wide range of security concepts through hands-on challenges.

This section contains notes, solutions, and strategies that will help us prepare for picoCTF, improve our problem-solving skills, and get ready for future competitions.
